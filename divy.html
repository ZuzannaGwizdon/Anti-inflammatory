BERRIES

Berries, whether they’re blueberries, strawberries, raspberries or blackberries.

All contain antioxidants known as anthocyanins. In fact, it’s the presence of anthocyanins that give berries their vibrant red, ue and purple color. While all fruits are generally high in antioxidants, berries are superstars with chemical compounds that great at fighting inflammation, cancer and cardiovascular disease. But here’s the cool part. Not only do berries reduce existing inflammation, but they train our cells to respond better to any episodes of future inflammation. And that’s why it’s important to eat them regularly.
